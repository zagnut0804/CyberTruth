Macbook Air: More Power in the Same Lightweight Design!


The new Macbook Air is here with a familiar case and upgraded specs. The upgraded processor, increased ram and faster M.2 storage provide a much needed boost in performance from last years model. It also offers an impressive 13 hours of batttery life during normal usage. The Macbook Air is available in Gold, Space Gray and Silver. The standard model includes 128GB of storage but it can be upgraded to 1TB. This years model comes highly recommended!
